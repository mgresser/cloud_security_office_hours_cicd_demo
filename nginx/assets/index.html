Why Office Hours?

Cloud Security needs more people! There are so many talented people out there who have skills that easily translate to the cloud. Our aim is to engage those who are interested in cloud security with those who have been in the field for years. Our weekly sessions bring together experts and novices for a one hour open forum for questions, stories, demonstrations, etc.

Some guidelines...

For those of you who are new to Office Hours, we invite anybody to join, but I do ask that everyone follow a few guidelines.

  • Please be respectful. There will be people from all knowledge levels, and we want to encourage all participation.

  • THERE ARE NO DUMB QUESTIONS. Please feel free to ask about anything that you think is related to Cloud Security. If you're thinking it, it's likely someone else is, too. Speak up!

  • Anybody is eligible to ask or respond to a question. Speak up or put something in chat, whatever you are comfortable with. And if you see or hear a question and want to speak up, please do so!

  • Also, this is a non-compete space for all vendors (this is important). We are all here to learn and share. This is not about products. But you might hear about products as it's the nature of our industry. Opinions are welcome, but we are not here to pitch anything.


These sessions are NOT recorded. I do not use ANY information from these sessions for marketing purposes, and that’s a promise.
